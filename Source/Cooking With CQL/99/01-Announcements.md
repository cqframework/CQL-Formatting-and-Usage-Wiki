## US Realm Connectathon

Virtual Connectathon, March 31st - April 2nd

[Registration](https://hl7.my.site.com/LightningMemberPortal/s/lt-event?id=a1YVM00000YGedp2AD)

[FHIR Quality Reporting with DEQM](https://confluence.hl7.org/spaces/FHIR/pages/413255255/2026+-+03+FHIR+Quality+Reporting+with+DEQM)

Focusing in particular on:

1. Provider Data Submission
2. Invited Pull
3. Using US Common

Recordings and slides for Track Kickoff and Planning Calls are available on the track page.

Content Sources:
* [Draft FHIR-based 2025 AU dQMs](https://github.com/cqframework/dqm-content-qicore-2025): Draft CMS dQM Content using QICore 6.0.0. This is the same content as the FHIR Public Comment
* [Exploratory FHIR-based 2026 AU dQMs](https://github.com/cqframework/dqm-content-qicore-2026): Draft CMS dQM Content, this content supports the advanced test cases (value-based stratifiers, supporting evidence, risk-adjusted measures)
* [Exploratory FHIR-based 2025 AU dQMs](https://github.com/cqframework/dqm-content-cms-2025): Draft CMS dQM Content using USCore 6.1.0-derived and QICore 6.0.0-derived.
